About

Slash is a cryptic hunt being organized by IEEE IIITD in collaboration with Women in Technology.

2 days. 36 hours. Starting at 12PM IST on the 24th of October. Players from all over the world will be presented with text-based questions of increasing difficulty at each level. The answer's hunt will take them all over the internet.
